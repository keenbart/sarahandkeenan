Wedding Details

Where: Chicago Botanic Garden
1000 Lake Cook Rd, Glencoe, IL 60022
When: Saturday, October 6, 2018 at 5 p.m.
Attire: Garden Party Cocktail
DRESS FOR THE WEATHER

Please dress for the weather, as we are hoping to have the ceremony and cocktail hour outside, and the dinner will be in the McGinley Pavilion (a tented space with heating, if needed). There will be a bit of a walk from the Botanic Garden entrance to the Rose Garden for the ceremony, and you will be walking across a grassy expanse in the Rose Garden, so choose your footwear accordingly.

INFO FOR OUT-OF-TOWN GUESTS

If you are traveling to Chicago from out of town, or if you are local but would like to ride our shuttle from downtown Chicago to the Botanic Garden and back, take a look at our Visiting Chicago page for more information.

FRIDAY NIGHT GATHERING

If you are in town and available on Friday night October 5 after dinner, please feel free to stop by Formento’s, 925 West Randolph, from 8:30pm-10:30pm to meet the wedding party and families, and to have some sweets and coffee. If anyone is seeking something a little stronger, a cash bar will be open for your drinking pleasure.

SUNDAY MORNING BRUNCH

We would also like to invite all out-of-town guests to brunch on Sunday, October 7, at the Booth One restaurant in the Ambassador Hotel, 1301 N State Pkwy. We will have a brunch buffet from 10:30am-12pm, so please come join us for a casual, day-after get together before heading home. (Please note, if you are not staying at the Ambassador or the Talbott, check the Marathon map to make sure you find a route that won’t be blocked.) Please RSVP here if you will be able to come to the brunch on Sunday morning.
